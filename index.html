Partnerships
NWVBUG Development operates under parent company Lye Sofware. Lye Software acts as the face of many of the consumer-facing products and operates the Lye Account System, which is used in NWVBUG products. NWVBUG Development has a sister company RyCorp, which also operates under Lye Software. RyCorp specializes in video games and Artificial Intelligence, and as such, NWVBUG and RyCorp often collaborate on AI projects.
