about

We are independent. We believe in data privacy and equal access to technology, which is why we host all of our services ourselves.

We believe that education should be free for all. It should not come at the cost of your money, privacy, or data.

We believe that your ability to talk to people should not be restricted by your internet speed.

We believe that your technology should help you.
